Chronological Intelligence & Mapping

Designed for scanned archives, photo albums, and old digital memories.

Chronological Interpolation

If a page or photograph has no date written, Pinmage automatically inherits the date of the previous chronological file to keep photos in perfect chronological order.

CoreLocation Geocoding

Pinmage leverages Apple's native geocoding engine to automatically turn place names identified by Gemini into real GPS coordinates (Latitude & Longitude).

Privacy-First & Secure

No account signups. Pinmage runs completely locally on your Mac, utilizing direct sandbox read/write tunnels. Your images never leave your machine.
